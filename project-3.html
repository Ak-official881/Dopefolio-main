Project Overview

The "Automatic Vehicle Rescue and Speed Management System" aims to revolutionize road safety by integrating intelligent automation into vehicles. This innovative system is designed to monitor and manage vehicle speeds, ensuring compliance with speed limits and enhancing overall driving safety. In emergencies, the system automatically alerts rescue services, providing real-time location data for swift assistance. By combining speed management and emergency response, the project seeks to reduce accidents and improve response times, ultimately creating a safer driving environment.

This advanced technology represents a significant step forward in automotive safety, leveraging automation to protect drivers and passengers alike.
